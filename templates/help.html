📘 Smart Expense Tracker - User Guide

1. Register / Login

Create an account or login using your username and password.

2. Add Expense

Go to "Add Expense" and enter amount, category and date.

3. View History

Check all your past expenses under "History".

4. Search

Use search to quickly find your transactions.

5. Dashboard

View summary and graphs of your expenses.

6. Calculator

Use calculator to calculate amounts before saving.

7. Tips

  • Track expenses daily
  • Use categories properly
  • Check dashboard weekly

🚀 Getting Started (Follow These Steps)

  1. 💰 Add Your Income
    Start by recording your income so you know how much money you have.
  2. 📊 Set Your Budget
    Plan how much you want to spend in different categories.
  3. 📝 Add Expenses
    Record your daily expenses (food, transport, etc).
  4. 📈 Check Dashboard
    View graphs and summaries of your spending.
  5. 🔍 Use Search & History
    Track and review your past transactions.
  6. 🧮 Use Calculator
    Calculate amounts before entering expenses.

⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Not adding income first
  • Forgetting to track daily expenses
  • Not checking dashboard regularly
